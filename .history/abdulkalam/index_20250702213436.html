Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India. He played a pivotal role in India's space and missile programs and was known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his work on the country's ballistic missile development.
