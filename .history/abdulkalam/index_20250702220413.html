A . P . J Abdul Kalam

1932-2015

Missile Man of India

Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, which established India as a nuclear power. Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, Dr. Kalam inspired millions with his vision, humility, and dedication to the nation.

Biography

  • Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • Graduated in Physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.
  • Worked at ISRO and DRDO, contributing to India's space and missile programs.
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Authored several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds".
